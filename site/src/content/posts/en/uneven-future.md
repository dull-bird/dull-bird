The future is already here, just unevenly distributed. I read that as a nice quote — until I started using AI every day to write code, read papers, translate essays.

## The gap isn't time

Same model: one person ships a prototype in ten minutes, another is still deciding whether to sign up. The tech is public; the **willingness to reach** is not.

> The future is already here — it's just not evenly distributed.

The slow bird starts early. Rather than wait for tech to spread, share the uses, the ideas, the potholes now.

## So I built this little place

Friendly to people, and friendly to AI agents — clean structure, clear semantics, a machine-readable view alongside. You can read it; so can your agent.
